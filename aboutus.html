Who we are

Sharp iTech has been formed with a very clear goal, to improve the end-to-end processes of shipping and logistics. The foundation blocks were laid with the resolve to map, analyse, simplify and redefine the processes to digitize and automate the system- reducing the data duplication- enabling the smart-data access routes and reducing the turn-around time per processes- thereby, reducing overall logistics EXIM costs.

Our unique distinction is our expertise that we have gained from over 18 years of on field experience. iCMS, the company’s flagship product, which is a web-based portal developed on a niche technology stack within-built AI and Machine learning capabilities is a one-stop solution for end-to-end process handing of the shipping/ logistics ecosystem. This includes digital quote manager, sales negotiation, booking lifecycle manager, container movement tracking, yard manager, Inventory Plan and Movement manager, EXIM doc manager, Global Agency management, Legal claims manager, Revenue Management and auto-invoicing, to name the ones that top our exquisite menu of offerings.

Vision

To be the most preferred and reliable technology partner for 360-degree ecosystem of Logistics Industry around the globe. Let our IT innovations transform the way, we do logistics today.

Mission

Developing cloud-based, AI-ML enabled ecosystem of logistics which is simple, economical, adaptable, sustainable, scalable and responsive to customers’ core business needs.
